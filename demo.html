About Us

"The ideal engineer is a composite. He is not a scientist, he is not a mathematician, he is not a sociologist or a writer, but he may use the knowledge and techniques of any or all of these disciplines in solving engineering problems" N. W. Dougherty.

Introduction:

Welcome to the Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering Department (EXTC) of Fr. C. Rodrigues Institute of Technology, Vashi, Navi Mumbai. All the Visions and Missions are HERE.

Electronics and telecommunication engineering is one of the largest and fastest-growing fields of engineering. Having qualified and trained professionals in this field are the need of the hour. Currently, the department offers:

  1. The Bachelor of Engineering (BE) degree (undergraduate)
  2. The Ph.D. degree in Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering

This department was established during the inception of the institute in 1994. Further details regarding UG course contents, department strength, achievements, etc. are available in "Brochure" which can be downloaded from the link available at the end of this document.

Student-Alumni Mentoring Platform:

Welcome to FCRIT (EXTC) STUDENT-ALUMNI MENTORING PLATFORM, a one-of-a-kind platform designed to bridge the gap between students and alumni, fostering meaningful connections and lifelong mentorship opportunities. Our mission is to empower students with guidance, knowledge, and inspiration from experienced alumni who have walked the same path. By creating a collaborative environment, we aim to help students achieve their academic, career, and personal growth goals while enabling alumni to give back to their alma mater in a truly impactful way.

Roles

Student

Students get the opportunity to be part of real-life projects and demo projects during their course of study, thereby helping them acquire technical experience.

Mentor

A mentor is an experienced and trusted advisor who provides guidance, and expertise to help individuals achieve their personal or professional goals. Help mentees set and achieve goals.

Teacher

A teacher educates, guides, and inspires students to reach their full potential. They create a positive learning environment that fosters intellectual, emotional, and social growth.
